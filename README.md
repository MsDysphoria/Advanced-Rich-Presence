## Information
`What is it?` <br />
- Advanced Rich Presence is a software that allows you to create, customize, and automate your Discord Rich Presence. You can set custom statuses, display animated or interactive content, and update your activity automatically in real time.

`Where can I use it?` <br />
- Originally designed to enhance Discord presence for personal expression or community engagement, it can be used by gamers, content creators, or anyone looking to showcase unique statuses and activities on Discord.

`How can I use it?` <br />
- Connect your Discord account and paste your Application ID from the Discord Developer Portal. Customize your Rich Presence with text, images, animations, or interactive buttons. Enable the display of current activity as a status message in Discord Activity Settings. You can also schedule updates or automate changes to reflect in-game progress, streaming activity, or custom events.
